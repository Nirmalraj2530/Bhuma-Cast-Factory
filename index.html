DO YOU KNOW
ABOUT BCF?

During our tenure with the family company, we have gained in depth knowledge on all facets of Foundry Engineering. We have been associated with various corporate entities as OEM supplier to different segment of engineering industry including Automotive, Agriculture engineering, equipment manufacturing enterprises, supply to infrastructure industry Equipment makers etc.

Even though the Enterprise is a new one it has its origin in one of the pioneering family of Casting Industry of Chennai, the Foundry which is in continuous operation since 1884. The promoter is one of the direct descendants of the family and has hands on experience built through more than 3 decades of experience gained through blood, sweat and tears by being always at the front line in the various phases of operation of the plant from concept to completion of finished product as per Customer's indent.
